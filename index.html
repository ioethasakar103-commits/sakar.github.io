About Me

Who I am

I’m a Civil Engineering Graduate (BCE) from Thapathali Campus, with +2 from Golden Gate International College. I enjoy working on structural systems where performance matters—especially steel frames and connections under seismic loading.

My work style is detail-oriented: I like to compare results, validate assumptions, and present findings in a clear “engineer-to-engineer” format.

What I do

  • Model and compare rigid vs semi-rigid beam–column connection behavior
  • Extract and interpret joint stiffness and moment–rotation response
  • Seismic response checks: drift, torsion, internal forces, and design implications
  • Create clean drawings and presentable visuals for reports and publications

Research • Seismic

Comparative evaluation of connection behavior (rigid vs semi-rigid) using global frame analysis and detailed joint modeling. Focus areas include rotational stiffness, moment–rotation response, and performance indicators relevant to seismic design decisions.

  • Tools: ETABS, IDEA StatiCa, Excel
  • Output: comparison plots, stiffness interpretation, report-ready tables
